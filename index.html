VISIT BAALBEK

In the modern era, Baalbek enjoys economic advantages as a sought-after tourist destination. It is known for the ruins of the Roman temple complex, which includes the Temple of Bacchus and the Temple of Jupiter, and was inscribed in 1984 as a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Top three activities to do at BAALBEK

Visit the Temples of Baalbek

Baalbek is renowned for its impressive Roman ruins, particularly the Temples of Jupiter and Bacchus. These temples are among the largest and best-preserved Roman temples in the world!

Explore the Stone of the Pregnant Woman

This massive stone block, located near the Temple complex, is a testament to the scale of construction in ancient Baalbek. It is believed to be one of the largest building stones ever quarried and transported!

Experience the Baalbeck International Festival

If your visit coincides with the festival, it's a must-see. The festival, held in the Roman temples, features a variety of cultural performances, including music concerts and theatrical productions.
